Run Eco-AI on Your Device

Experience decentralized AI that respects your privacy and helps save the planet by running directly on your device. Follow the steps below to get started.

Overview

Eco-AI lets you run AI models locally without relying on large centralized servers, reducing energy consumption and increasing privacy.

Setup Instructions

  1. Download the Eco-AI client application from here.
  2. Install the application on your device following the on-screen instructions.
  3. Configure your preferences and start running AI tasks locally.

FAQ

Q: Is my data safe?

A: Yes, all processing is done locally on your device, so your data never leaves it.

Q: What devices are supported?

A: Eco-AI currently supports Windows, macOS, and Linux desktops.
