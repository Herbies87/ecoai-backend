What is Eco-AI and Why Is It Better?

What is Eco-AI?

Eco-AI is a decentralized artificial intelligence system that runs on a network of consumer-owned nodes using everyday devices, rather than massive centralized data centers. This approach drastically reduces energy consumption and environmental impact.

Why is Eco-AI better than traditional AI?

Traditional AI relies on huge, power-hungry data centers often powered by non-renewable energy sources. Eco-AI prioritizes sustainability by distributing workloads to greener devices, reducing carbon footprint while maintaining performance.

How does Eco-AI route customer queries?

We intelligently route customer queries and AI responses to the most eco-friendly servers available. This means considering not just speed but also power sources like solar, wind, nuclear, or coal. Our system continuously monitors the energy profiles of nodes, including the type of power source and current availability, to make real-time routing decisions that maximize use of renewable energy.
